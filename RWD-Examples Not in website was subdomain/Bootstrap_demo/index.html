    It All Began in 1985

    when a local Chicago boy named Chef Bobby perfected his family’s recipe for a sweet and tangy BBQ sauce and entered it into the country’s largest rib cook-off.

    Chef Bobby called his sauce Sweet Baby Ray’s after his little brother Billy, who got the nickname shootin’ hoops on the west side of Chicago. On the day of the rib-off, Sweet Baby Ray’s beat nearly 500 entrants — an amazing feat for an unknown. The rest, as they say, is history.
